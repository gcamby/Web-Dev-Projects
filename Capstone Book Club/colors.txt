Color Schemes

Deep Wine Red (#8B2635): This rich, deep red evokes the warmth and depth of a good book.
It can be used for headings, buttons, and other focal points.

Creamy Beige (#F5F1E3): This soft, warm beige works well as a background color, providing
a light and airy feel that contrasts nicely with deeper tones.

Midnight Blue (#1B3A57): A deep blue that suggests reliability and depth. It's excellent
for text or to highlight active menu items.

Soft Gold (#CBA135): This muted gold can add a touch of elegance and highlight important
features like quotes or keynotes.

Dusty Rose (#D7A9A9): A muted pink that offers a touch of warmth and friendliness, 
perfect for secondary buttons or highlights.
